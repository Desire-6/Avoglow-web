Description

Root & Revive is a nourishing hair elixir crafted from avocado oil, castor oil and botanical extracts to help strengthen hair, support healthy growth and restore shine. It deeply moisturizes the scalp while helping reduce dryness and breakage.

Key Benefits

  • Nourishes the scalp
  • Supports healthier-looking roots
  • Helps reduce hair dryness
  • Supports stronger-looking hair
  • Suitable for natural and protective styles

How To Use

  • Apply directly to the scalp.
  • Massage gently for 2–5 minutes.
  • Focus on thinning areas, edges and damaged hair.
  • Use 3–4 times per week or as needed.

Key Ingredients

  • Infused Avocado Oil(rosemary, moringa, fenugreek, hibiscus, black seed, cloves, coriander, bay leaves.)
  • Jamaican Black Castor Oil
  • Jojoba Oil
  • Rosemary Essential Oil
  • Lavender Essential Oil
  • Cedar wood Essential Oil
  • Peppermint Essential Oil

The Story Behind Root & Revive

Every bottle begins with avocados sourced through Atari Farms. Instead of allowing valuable avocado resources to go to waste, we transform them into nourishing wellness products that create value for farmers while delivering natural care for your hair.
