OUR PROCESS

The Circular Journey

Every avocado follows a journey that creates value for people, communities, and the environment. From the farm to premium beauty products and future innovations, nothing valuable should go to waste.

01

Farmers Grow Avocados

Smallholder farmers around Mount Elgon carefully cultivate avocado trees that improve household incomes while helping protect the landscape from soil erosion.

02

We Purchase the Fruit

Avoglow purchases fresh avocados directly from farming communities, creating a reliable market and ensuring farmers receive value for their harvests.

03

Premium Cold-Pressed Oil

Fresh avocados are transformed into premium cold-pressed avocado oil, preserving the nutrients that nourish healthy hair and skin.

04

Beauty Products

The avocado oil becomes the heart of our premium beauty collection including Root & Revive Hair Elixir, Intensive Hair Butter, Hydration Mist and future skincare products.

05

Avocado Seeds Become AvoChai

Rather than becoming waste, avocado seeds are transformed into AvoChai, our caffeine-free wellness drink that creates even more value from every fruit.

06

What's Next

We're exploring new ways to transform avocado pomace into environmentally friendly products such as briquettes and other value-added materials. Nothing valuable should go to waste.

OUR IMPACT

Why It Matters

Every avocado creates value beyond beauty. Our circular approach benefits farmers, protects nature, reduces waste, and delivers premium natural products to consumers.

Farmers

Reliable markets create additional income for farming families while strengthening rural livelihoods around Mount Elgon.

Environment

Greater value encourages communities to preserve avocado trees that protect the Mount Elgon ecosystem and reduce soil erosion.

Circular Economy

Every part of the avocado is put to good use, reducing waste while creating new opportunities for innovation.

Consumers

Carefully selected avocado ingredients are transformed into premium natural products that nourish hair, skin and wellness.
