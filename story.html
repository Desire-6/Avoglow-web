ABOUT AVOGLOW

Turning Avocados Into Wellness

Avoglow was created to transform the hidden potential of avocados into products that nourish people and protect the environment. Inspired by nature and powered by innovation, we craft premium haircare, skincare and wellness products from avocado fruits and seeds that would otherwise go underutilized.

Our approach combines natural ingredients, sustainable sourcing and a commitment to quality, creating products that support healthier lifestyles while contributing to a more responsible future. Through every product, Avoglow seeks to deliver wellness, celebrate the value of nature and create positive impact across communities.

ATARI FARMS CONNECTION

From Farm Innovation To Natural Wellness

Avoglow was born from the experience, innovation and farmer partnerships developed through Atari Farms.

Working closely with avocado-growing communities revealed opportunities to create more value from every harvest while supporting sustainable livelihoods.

Today, Avoglow transforms that vision into premium avocado-powered wellness and beauty products inspired by nature and designed for modern lifestyles.

Powered by the agricultural expertise, innovation and farmer networks of Atari Farms.
FOUNDER STORY

Turning Agricultural Challenges Into Opportunities

Avoglow began with a simple belief: every harvest should create opportunity, not waste.

Through Atari Farms, we witnessed the untapped potential of avocados and the opportunities that could be created by transforming agricultural by-products into valuable wellness solutions.

Today, Avoglow continues that vision by creating natural products that support healthier lifestyles while creating sustainable opportunities for farming communities.

"Every harvest holds untapped potential."

Idi Mukasa Bate, Founder & CEO
