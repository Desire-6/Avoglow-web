Description

AvoChai is a wholesome wellness beverage made from carefully processed avocado seeds blended with warming spices. Created as a caffeine-free alternative to conventional tea and coffee, AvoChai offers a rich, satisfying flavor that can be enjoyed morning, afternoon, or evening. Every cup reflects Avoglow's commitment to transforming avocado resources into innovative products that support wellness and sustainability.

Key Benefits

  • Caffeine-free alternative to tea and coffee
  • Made from avocado seeds
  • Rich and warming flavor profile
  • Suitable for any time of day
  • Crafted from natural ingredients

How To Use

  • Add 1–2teaspoons of Avochai to hot water.
  • Allow to steep for 5–10.
  • Strain if desired.
  • Enjoy hot.

    • Optional:

  • Add honey or milk according to preference.

Key Ingredients

  • Avocado Seed powder
  • Ginger
  • Cinnamon
  • Cardamom
  • Cloves

The Story Behind Avochai

AvoChai was born from Avoglow's mission to ensure that every part of the avocado creates value. By transforming avocado seeds into a flavorful wellness beverage, we developed a product that supports sustainability while offering consumers a unique and enjoyable caffeine-free drink. Every cup of AvoChai represents innovation, wellness, and responsible resource utilization.
