Description

Intensive Hair Butter is a rich blend of nourishing butters and oils designed to help moisturize, soften and protect dry, fragile hair. It helps seal in moisture, improve softness, reduce breakage and support length retention by protecting the hair from excessive dryness.

Key Benefits

  • Deeply moisturizes dry hair
  • Helps reduce breakage
  • Supports length retention
  • Improves softness and manageability
  • Helps protect fragile strands

How To Use

  • Apply to damp or hydrated hair.
  • Focus on hair strands and ends.
  • Use after applying Hydration Mist and Root & Revive.
  • Helps seal in moisture and reduce breakage.

Key Ingredients

  • Shea Butter
  • Mango Butter
  • Beeswax
  • Infused Avocado Oil
  • Jojoba Oil
  • Castor Oil
  • Rosemary essential oil
  • Lavender essential oil
  • Cedar wood essential Oil
  • Peppermint essential Oil

The Story Behind Intensive Hair Butter

Intensive Hair Butter was created to transform avocado resources into effective wellness products that nourish both people and communities. By combining the richness of avocado oil with carefully selected botanical ingredients, we developed a treatment that restores moisture, strengthens hair, and celebrates the power of natural care.
